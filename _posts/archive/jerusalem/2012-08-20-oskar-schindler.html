The grave of Oskar Schindler. Located in a Christian graveyard in Jerusalem, this grave is one of the most visited and venerated tombs by Jews in Jerusalem. Rocks placed on the tomb symbolize respect and reverence, placed there for the same reason others often place flowers on tombstones in other cultures.
